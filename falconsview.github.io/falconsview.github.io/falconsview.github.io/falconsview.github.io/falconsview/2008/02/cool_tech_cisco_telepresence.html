Cool Tech: Cisco TelePresence

Regardless of how you feel about Cisco, you have to check out some of the brochures for their TelePresence solutions. This isn't just a monitor+camera+audio solution, but a complete environment. Imagine is full 1080p high def, the audio is directional and stereo, meaning the sound will come from the direction that the speaker visually appears on the screen. Etc. No idea how well it works, but it looks and sounds really, really cool. :)
